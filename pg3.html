Sunshine Resort: Guest Satisfaction & Booking Trend Analysis

Uncovering insights to elevate the guest experience

Sunshine Resort, a luxurious getaway destination, was eager to enhance guest satisfaction and optimize booking patterns. I analyzed historical booking data and guest feedback using Excel to identify seasonality trends, top-performing room types, and pain points in the guest experience. The analysis highlighted peak booking periods, common complaints, and factors influencing repeat visits. These insights helped the resort adjust staffing during high-demand seasons and improve room service protocols, leading to increased positive reviews and return bookings.
